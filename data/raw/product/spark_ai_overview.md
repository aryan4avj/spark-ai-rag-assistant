# Spark AI Overview

Spark AI is an internal knowledge assistant designed to help employees find answers across technical documents, architecture notes, and product guidance.

## Primary use cases

Primary use cases include answering implementation questions, reducing time spent searching documentation, and improving cross-team access to institutional knowledge.

## Important design goals

Important design goals include grounded answers, source transparency, fast retrieval, and maintainable ingestion pipelines.
