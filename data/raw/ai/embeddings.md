# Embeddings Explained

Embeddings are dense numeric representations of text that capture semantic meaning.

## Why embeddings matter

Embeddings allow semantically similar text to be placed close together in vector space. This enables similarity search.

## Limitations

Embeddings are useful for meaning-based search, but they do not replace exact keyword matching. Terms like error codes, IDs, and policy names may still need lexical search.
